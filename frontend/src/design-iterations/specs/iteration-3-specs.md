## üìù Typography

- **Font:** Inter (primary), Plus Jakarta Sans (headings)
- **Weights:** Normal to Semibold (no extrabold)
- **Line Height:** 1.6-1.8 (more relaxed, readable)
- **Style:** Friendly, approachable, softer

---

## üî≤ Design Characteristics

### Borders
- **Softer, rounder:** Larger border radius (12-24px)
- **Gentle:** Thinner borders (1px)
- **Organic shapes:** Pills, rounded rectangles

### Shadows
- **Soft and diffuse:** More blur, lighter colors
- **Colored shadows:** Green-tinted shadows on primary elements
- **Gentle elevation:** Subtle depth

### Cards
- **Rounded corners:** 16px+ border radius
- **Soft shadows:** Large blur radius
- **Gradient accents:** Subtle green-to-teal gradients
- **Warm backgrounds:** Cream/warm gray tones
