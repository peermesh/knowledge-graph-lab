## 📝 Research Brief Templates

**Before you start**: Read the [Research Methodology Guide](./docs/research/research-methodology.md) to learn the SEARCH framework, and the [Research Process Guide](./docs/research/research-process.md) for practical execution steps.

### Standard Template Structure

Your research brief should follow this 2-page structure:

```markdown
# Module [X] Research Brief: [Module Title]
**Intern**: [Your Name]  
**Date**: [Submission Date]  
**Due**: Friday 5:00 PM Week 1

## Executive Summary (300 words)
- Focus question response
- Top 3 technology recommendations
- Key risks and mitigation strategies

## Analysis Framework (800 words)
### Technology Comparison
- Detailed evaluation of 3-5 options
- Scoring matrix (1-5 scale) for key criteria
- Justification for recommendations

### Implementation Timeline
- Development phases with AI assistance multipliers
- Risk mitigation strategies
- Dependency management plan

### Integration Assessment
- How your module connects to others
- Data flow and API design considerations
- Testing and validation approach

## Deliverables Summary (200 words)
- Technology recommendation matrix
- Implementation timeline
- Risk assessment with fallbacks
- Integration complexity assessment

## Questions & Next Steps (200 words)
- Areas needing clarification
- Preparation for Week 2 planning
- Resource requirements
```

### Evaluation Criteria

Your research brief will be evaluated on:

| Criteria | Weight | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| **Technical Depth** | 30% | Quality of technology analysis and comparisons |
| **Implementation Focus** | 25% | Practicality of recommendations for 10-week timeline |
| **Risk Assessment** | 20% | Identification and mitigation of key risks |
| **Integration Awareness** | 15% | Understanding of module interactions |
| **Communication** | 10% | Clarity, structure, and professional presentation |

**Grading Scale**:
- **A (90-100%)**: Exceptional analysis, clear recommendations, comprehensive risk assessment
- **B (80-89%)**: Solid analysis, good recommendations, adequate risk coverage
- **C (70-79%)**: Basic analysis, reasonable recommendations, some risk considerations
- **Below 70%**: Requires revision and resubmission

## 💡 Tips for Success

### Research Strategy
- **Start broad, then narrow**: Get the landscape view before diving deep
- **Focus on implementation**: Academic papers are interesting, but practical solutions matter most
- **Consider the timeline**: 10 weeks is short - prioritize MVPs over perfect solutions
- **Think integration**: Your module doesn't exist in isolation

### Time Management
- **Front-load research**: Week 1 research determines everything else
- **Ask questions early**: Don't struggle alone - help is available
- **Document decisions**: You'll reference your research throughout development
- **Plan for unknowns**: Always have backup options

### Communication Best Practices
- **Be specific**: "It doesn't work" → "FastAPI returns 500 error when POST /entities"  
- **Share context**: Include error messages, system info, what you tried
- **Update status**: Let team know when you're blocked or making progress
- **Help others**: Share useful resources and insights you discover
