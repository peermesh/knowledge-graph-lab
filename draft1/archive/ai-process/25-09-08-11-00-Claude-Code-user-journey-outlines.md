## User Types & Primary Goals

### 1. **Researchers** (Academic/Industry Analysts)
**Primary Goal**: Deep understanding of creator economy trends, patterns, and relationships

### 2. **Content Creators** (Individual Creators/Creator Teams)  
**Primary Goal**: Discover opportunities, understand platform landscape, find funding

### 3. **Policy Makers** (Government/Regulatory Bodies)
**Primary Goal**: Track policy impact, understand regulatory needs, monitor industry changes

### 4. **Investors** (VCs/Angels/Funds)
**Primary Goal**: Market intelligence, opportunity identification, competitive analysis

### 5. **Platform Operators** (YouTube/TikTok/Patreon/etc)
**Primary Goal**: Competitive intelligence, ecosystem understanding, partnership opportunities

### 6. **Advocates** (Creator Rights Organizations)
**Primary Goal**: Policy tracking, organizing resources, community building

## Core User Journey Outlines

### Journey 1: Grant Discovery & Application
**User**: Content Creator  
**Trigger**: Creator needs funding for a project  
**Outcome**: Successfully identifies and applies to relevant grants

**Journey Steps**:
1. **Describe Project** → Input project type, creator category, location
2. **Discover Grants** → System finds matching grant opportunities  
3. **Evaluate Options** → Compare eligibility, amounts, deadlines, requirements
4. **Deep Dive** → Research grant providers, success rates, past recipients
5. **Application Prep** → Access application templates, requirements, contacts
6. **Track Progress** → Monitor application status, get deadline reminders

### Journey 2: Platform Ecosystem Research
**User**: Researcher  
**Trigger**: Analyzing creator platform landscape for report  
**Outcome**: Comprehensive understanding of platform relationships and market dynamics

**Journey Steps**:
1. **Define Research** → Specify platform categories, regions, timeframe
2. **Explore Platforms** → Browse platform database with filtering/search
3. **Analyze Relationships** → Understand ownership, partnerships, competitive dynamics  
4. **Track Changes** → Monitor acquisitions, policy changes, new features
5. **Export Insights** → Generate reports, citations, data visualizations
6. **Stay Updated** → Subscribe to relevant platform change notifications

### Journey 3: Policy Impact Monitoring  
**User**: Policy Maker  
**Trigger**: Need to understand impact of creator economy regulations  
**Outcome**: Clear picture of policy effects and future regulatory needs

**Journey Steps**:
1. **Set Parameters** → Define policy areas, jurisdictions, creator segments
2. **Monitor Changes** → Track new legislation, regulatory updates, court decisions
3. **Assess Impact** → Understand effects on creators, platforms, market dynamics
4. **Identify Gaps** → Find areas needing regulatory attention or clarification
5. **Generate Reports** → Create policy briefs for stakeholders
6. **Collaborate** → Share insights with other regulatory bodies

### Journey 4: Investment Opportunity Analysis
**User**: Investor  
**Trigger**: Evaluating creator economy investment opportunities  
**Outcome**: Data-driven investment decision with competitive intelligence

**Journey Steps**:
1. **Market Scan** → Identify emerging platforms, tools, creator segments
2. **Competitive Analysis** → Understand existing players, market positioning
3. **Trend Analysis** → Spot growth patterns, technological shifts, user behavior
4. **Risk Assessment** → Evaluate regulatory risks, platform dependencies
5. **Due Diligence** → Deep dive on specific opportunities with supporting data
6. **Portfolio Tracking** → Monitor investment performance and market changes

### Journey 5: Advocacy Campaign Research
**User**: Creator Rights Advocate  
**Trigger**: Building campaign for creator rights issue  
**Outcome**: Well-researched campaign with supporting data and coalition building

**Journey Steps**:
1. **Issue Definition** → Identify specific creator rights concern
2. **Evidence Gathering** → Collect data on impact, affected creators, precedents  
3. **Stakeholder Mapping** → Find allies, opponents, neutral parties, decision makers
4. **Campaign Strategy** → Develop messaging, tactics, timeline based on research
5. **Resource Assembly** → Gather supporting documents, expert contacts, case studies
6. **Coalition Building** → Connect with other advocates, creators, supportive organizations

### Journey 6: Creator Education & Onboarding
**User**: New Content Creator  
**Trigger**: Starting creator journey, needs comprehensive landscape overview  
**Outcome**: Educated creator with clear understanding of opportunities and resources

**Journey Steps**:
1. **Creator Assessment** → Input skills, interests, goals, resources
2. **Platform Guidance** → Understand platform options, pros/cons, requirements
3. **Monetization Education** → Learn revenue streams, pricing, business models
4. **Resource Discovery** → Find tools, services, communities, mentorship
5. **Legal/Business Basics** → Understand contracts, taxes, intellectual property
6. **Growth Planning** → Develop strategy with milestones and resource needs

### Journey 7: Competitive Intelligence Deep Dive
**User**: Platform Operator  
**Trigger**: Understanding competitive landscape for strategic planning  
**Outcome**: Comprehensive competitive intelligence for strategic decision making

**Journey Steps**:
1. **Competitive Scope** → Define direct/indirect competitors, market segments
2. **Feature Analysis** → Compare platform capabilities, creator tools, monetization
3. **Creator Migration** → Track creator movement between platforms, reasons
4. **Partnership Mapping** → Understand ecosystem partnerships, integrations
5. **Innovation Tracking** → Monitor new features, business model experiments  
6. **Strategic Planning** → Use insights for product roadmap, partnership decisions

### Journey 8: Academic Research Paper Development
**User**: Academic Researcher  
**Trigger**: Writing paper on creator economy topic  
**Outcome**: Well-sourced academic paper with comprehensive data backing

**Journey Steps**:
1. **Literature Review** → Find existing research, identify knowledge gaps
2. **Data Collection** → Gather quantitative/qualitative data from system
3. **Methodology Design** → Plan research approach using available data sources
4. **Analysis & Insights** → Process data, identify patterns, test hypotheses  
5. **Paper Writing** → Draft with proper citations, methodology, limitations
6. **Publication Prep** → Format for target journals, prepare supplementary data

## Cross-Journey Patterns

### Common Entry Points
1. **Search-Driven** → User has specific question or keyword
2. **Browse-Driven** → User exploring broadly within domain
3. **Alert-Driven** → User responding to notification/update
4. **Task-Driven** → User has specific goal (apply for grant, write report)

### Common System Interactions
1. **Filter & Search** → Narrow down large datasets to relevant items
2. **Relationship Exploration** → Understand connections between entities
3. **Deep Dive Research** → Get comprehensive information on specific topics
4. **Export & Share** → Take insights out of system for external use
5. **Subscribe & Monitor** → Stay updated on relevant changes
6. **Collaborate** → Share findings with team members or stakeholders

### Common Success Metrics
1. **Time to Insight** → How quickly users find relevant information
2. **Research Completeness** → Confidence that nothing important was missed  
3. **Action Enablement** → System provides enough info for next steps
4. **Update Relevance** → Notifications match user interests and timing needs
5. **Export Quality** → Exported data/reports meet professional standards

## Technical Requirements Derived from Journeys

### Data Requirements
- **Entities**: Creators, Platforms, Organizations, Grants, Policies, Events
- **Relationships**: Ownership, Partnerships, Funding, Regulatory Impact
- **Temporal Data**: Track changes over time for trend analysis
- **Geographic Data**: Location-based filtering and analysis

### Functional Requirements  
- **Advanced Search**: Multi-faceted search across all entity types
- **Relationship Visualization**: Graph views of ecosystem connections
- **Real-time Updates**: Notifications when relevant changes occur
- **Export Capabilities**: Professional reports, data downloads, citations
- **Collaboration Features**: Sharing, commenting, team workspaces

### Performance Requirements
- **Search Speed**: <500ms for complex queries across large datasets
- **Data Freshness**: Critical updates within 24 hours, full refresh weekly  
- **Concurrent Users**: Support 50+ researchers simultaneously
- **Export Speed**: Generate reports/exports within 60 seconds
