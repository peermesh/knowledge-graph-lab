### Common Issues

#### Can't find your assignment?
1. Check the [Project Board](https://github.com/grigb/knowledge-graph-lab-alpha-setup/projects/1)
2. Look for issue assigned to your module (#7-10)
3. Read your module's README in `/modules/module-X/`

#### Need clarification on research brief?
- Review the [Research Brief Template](research-brief-template.md)
- Check your module's `week-1-research.md` file
- Ask in your module's Slack channel

#### Technical setup problems?
- Follow the [Intern Guide](../INTERN-GUIDE.md) setup section
- Post specific error messages in Slack
- Attend Technical Help office hours

## Submission Process

### Week 1 Research Brief
**Due**: Friday 5:00 PM

1. Use the [Research Brief Template](research-brief-template.md)
2. Save as: `research-brief-[your-name].md`
3. Submit via GitHub:
   - Fork the repository
   - Add your brief to `/submissions/week-1/`
   - Create a Pull Request
   - Link PR in your assigned issue

### Getting Feedback
- All submissions reviewed within 24 hours
- Feedback posted as comments on your PR
- Revisions welcome until Monday morning
