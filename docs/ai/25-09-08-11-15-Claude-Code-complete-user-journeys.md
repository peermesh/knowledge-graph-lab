## Journey 1: Grant Discovery & Application

### User Profile: Sarah Chen - Independent Video Creator
**Background**: 28-year-old documentary filmmaker in Denver, Colorado. Creates environmental awareness videos. Has 50k YouTube subscribers but struggles with funding for bigger projects. Heard about grants but doesn't know where to start.

**Context**: Sarah wants to create a documentary about Colorado water rights but needs $15,000 for equipment, travel, and post-production.

**Goal**: Find and successfully apply for relevant grant funding

---

### Detailed Journey Map

#### Phase 1: Initial Discovery
**Step 1: Arrives at KGL Platform**
- **Action**: Discovers KGL through creator community recommendation
- **Emotion**: 😰 Overwhelmed - "There are grants for creators? Where do I even start?"
- **System Need**: Clear value proposition, easy onboarding

**Step 2: Profile Setup**  
- **Action**: Creates account, specifies creator type (video/documentary), location (Colorado), project focus (environmental)
- **Emotion**: 🤔 Cautious optimism - "Hope this actually helps"
- **System Response**: Personalization engine captures preferences

**Step 3: Initial Grant Search**
- **Action**: Searches "documentary grants Colorado environment" 
- **Emotion**: 😕 Frustration - "These results are too generic"
- **Pain Point**: Generic search results don't match specific needs
- **System Need**: Smart search that understands context and intent

#### Phase 2: Refined Discovery
**Step 4: Advanced Filtering**
- **Action**: Uses filters for amount ($10k-$20k), deadlines (next 6 months), eligibility (individual creators)
- **Emotion**: 🙂 Relief - "Now we're getting somewhere"
- **System Response**: Shows 12 relevant grants with eligibility scoring
- **Technical Requirement**: Multi-faceted filtering with eligibility matching

**Step 5: Grant Deep Dive**
- **Action**: Clicks on "Colorado Council on the Arts Media Grant"
- **Emotion**: 😮 Excitement - "This is perfect! But wait..."
- **System Response**: Shows full grant details, eligibility requirements, past recipients, success tips
- **Pain Point**: Uncertainty about eligibility - "Am I really qualified for this?"

**Step 6: Eligibility Validation**
- **Action**: Uses eligibility checker tool
- **Emotion**: 😌 Confidence - "Yes! I meet all the requirements"
- **System Response**: Green checkmarks on all eligibility criteria with explanations
- **Technical Requirement**: Rule engine for eligibility assessment

#### Phase 3: Application Preparation
**Step 7: Research Past Recipients**
- **Action**: Browses successful applications from similar creators
- **Emotion**: 📚 Motivated - "I can see exactly what worked"
- **System Response**: Shows anonymized successful applications, common themes, expert tips
- **Technical Requirement**: Content analysis of successful applications

**Step 8: Application Planning**
- **Action**: Downloads application template, creates project timeline
- **Emotion**: ✅ Organized - "I have a clear plan now"
- **System Response**: Provides templates, checklists, deadline tracking
- **Technical Requirement**: Document templates and project management tools

**Step 9: Expert Connection**
- **Action**: Connects with grant consultant through platform
- **Emotion**: 🤝 Supported - "Great to have expert guidance"
- **System Response**: Matches with consultants experienced in her grant type
- **Technical Requirement**: Expert network with matching algorithms

#### Phase 4: Application & Follow-up
**Step 10: Application Submission**
- **Action**: Submits application, uploads to KGL for tracking
- **Emotion**: 😬 Nervous excitement - "Here goes nothing!"
- **System Response**: Confirms submission, sets up tracking and reminders
- **Technical Requirement**: Application tracking and notification system

**Step 11: Ongoing Monitoring**
- **Action**: Receives weekly updates on similar grants, application status
- **Emotion**: 😊 Grateful - "This system keeps me informed without being pushy"

**Step 12: Success & Expansion**
- **Action**: Gets grant! Shares success story, discovers more advanced grants
