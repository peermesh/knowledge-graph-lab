## TASK 3: CONTINGENCY PLANNING

### **Technical Disruption Plans**

#### **Zoom/Platform Failure**
- **Primary**: Backup meeting room link prepared and tested
- **Secondary**: Phone conference bridge (numbers in agenda notes)
- **Prevention**: Test primary room 30 minutes before kickoff
- **Recovery Time**: < 2 minutes to move to backup

#### **GitHub Repository Access Issues**
- **Primary**: Pre-recorded screen recording of repository walkthrough (5 minutes)
- **Secondary**: PDF diagrams of repository structure for sharing
- **Prevention**: Test repository access with dummy account Sunday evening
- **Recovery Time**: Immediate transition to recorded walkthrough

#### **Internet Connectivity Problems**
- **Adaptation**: Meeting proceeds with discussion focus, skip live demos
- **Materials**: Key documents available as offline PDFs on presenter machine
- **Communication**: Cell phone numbers available for urgent coordination
- **Content Adjustment**: Emphasize Q&A and discussion over visual demonstrations

### **Attendance Issue Plans**

#### **Intern No-Show/Late Arrival**
- **Response**: Continue meeting on schedule (other 3 interns need information)
- **Individual Support**: Email complete materials package immediately
- **Follow-up**: Schedule 30-minute individual catchup within 24 hours
- **Documentation**: Record key sections for makeup viewing

#### **Project Lead/Mentor Absence**
- **Backup Presenter**: Technical mentor fully briefed and authorized
- **Materials**: All talking points documented and accessible to backup
- **Authority**: Backup presenter can make scope decisions and answer questions
- **Coordination**: Primary contact available via phone for critical decisions

### **Content/Process Issue Plans**

#### **Meeting Running Over Time**
**Priority Cut Strategy**:
1. **Never Cut**: Week 1 assignments (critical for Week 1 success)
2. **Never Cut**: Q&A (essential for clarification)
3. **First Cut**: Section 4 - Timeline detail (provide summary document instead)
4. **Second Cut**: Section 6 - GitHub walkthrough (send setup guide by email)
5. **Buffer Rule**: Start Q&A at 50-minute mark regardless of agenda position

#### **Overwhelming Questions/Confusion**
**Response Framework**:
- **Acknowledge**: "Excellent question - this shows good thinking"
- **Defer Complex**: "Perfect for office hours - let's dive deeper there"
- **Capture All**: Dedicated note-taker records every question for follow-up
- **Time Management**: "Let me capture that and circle back after the overview"

#### **Module Assignment Conflicts**
**Resolution Process**:
1. **Explain**: Assignments based on strategic background assessment
2. **Defer**: "Let's discuss your concerns in 1:1 follow-up"
3. **Emphasize**: All modules contribute equally to final success
4. **Compromise**: Module swap allowed within 48 hours if truly incompatible

### **Preparation Issue Plans**

#### **Materials Not Accessible During Meeting**
**Backup Materials Strategy**:
- **Digital**: USB drive with complete document set
- **Physical**: Printed copies of research brief templates (4 copies)
- **Cloud**: Documents accessible from multiple cloud accounts
- **Screen Share**: Local copies available for immediate sharing

#### **Mixed Experience Levels Among Interns**
**Adaptation Strategy**:
- **Emphasize**: Research phase levels the playing field (no coding Week 1)
- **Highlight**: Tiered implementation approach accommodates all skill levels
- **Support**: AI assistance acts as experience multiplier
- **Extra Help**: Additional office hours available for those needing more guidance
