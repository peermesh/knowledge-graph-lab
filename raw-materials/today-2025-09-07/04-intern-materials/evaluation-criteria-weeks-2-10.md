# Comprehensive Evaluation Criteria for Weeks 2-10

**Status**: ✅ COMPLETE - Detailed evaluation framework for entire program
**Purpose**: Clear, objective criteria for assessing intern progress and providing feedback

## Overall Evaluation Framework

### Grading Distribution
- **Weekly Progress** (40%): Consistent delivery and iteration
- **Code Quality** (30%): Technical excellence and best practices
- **Demo Performance** (20%): Communication and presentation skills
- **Collaboration** (10%): Team contribution and knowledge sharing

### Rating Scale (Applied to All Criteria)
- **5 - Exceptional**: Exceeds expectations, innovative solutions, helps others
- **4 - Strong**: Meets all requirements, solid implementation, proactive
- **3 - Satisfactory**: Meets basic requirements, occasional guidance needed
- **2 - Developing**: Below expectations, significant guidance required
- **1 - Unsatisfactory**: Major gaps, blocking issues, disengaged

## Performance Improvement Process

### If Behind Schedule (Triggered at Week 3, 5, or 7)
1. **Immediate 1:1 scheduled** within 24 hours
2. **Root cause analysis** conducted together
3. **Adjusted scope** defined with clear priorities
4. **Daily check-ins** established until caught up
5. **Peer mentor** assigned if needed

### Support Escalation Path
- **Level 1**: Peer assistance (teammate help)
- **Level 2**: Technical mentor guidance
- **Level 3**: Project lead intervention
- **Level 4**: Scope reduction discussion
